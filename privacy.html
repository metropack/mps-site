Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 28, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how MPS Inc (“we,” “us,” “our”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use our website, the Print Shop Invoice web app, and our downloadable desktop app (together, the “Services”).

Information We Collect

  • Account data: name, email, company, password hash.
  • Billing data: handled by our payment processor (e.g., Stripe) — we do not store full card numbers.
  • Usage data: device, browser, IP address, pages viewed, actions, crash logs.
  • Customer content: estimates, invoices, line items, uploaded logos/proofs.
  • Cookies: for authentication and session management.

How We Use Information

  • Provide, secure, and improve the Services.
  • Authenticate users and prevent fraud/abuse.
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions.
  • Provide support and communicate service updates.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

Sharing

We share data only as needed with service providers (e.g., hosting, analytics, payments) under agreements requiring appropriate safeguards. We may disclose information to comply with law, protect rights, or as part of a merger/acquisition.

Data Retention

We retain information while your account is active and as needed for legitimate business or legal purposes. You can request deletion (subject to legal/recordkeeping obligations).

Your Choices

  • You can access/update certain info in your account.
  • You can request deletion of your account by contacting [support@printshopinvoice.com].
  • You can control cookies in your browser; disabling may impact functionality.

Security

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect data. No system is 100% secure.

International Transfers

Your data may be processed in the United States and other countries where our providers operate.

Children’s Privacy

The Services are not directed to children under 13 (or as defined by local law). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

GDPR/CPRA Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or port your data, and to object or restrict certain processing. Contact us to exercise these rights.

Changes

We will post updates here and revise the “Last updated” date. Material changes may be communicated by email or in-app.
