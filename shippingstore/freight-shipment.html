Freight Checklist

Review the checklist below before requesting a quote. You can also download a checklist file for your records.

Information to Gather

  • Full name, company name, phone number, and email address
  • Pickup city, state, zip code, and whether it is residential or commercial
  • Delivery city, state, zip code, and whether it is residential or commercial
  • Number of pallets or pieces
  • Dimensions for each pallet: length, width, and height
  • Weight per pallet and total shipment weight
  • Commodity description
  • Packaging type: palletized, boxed, crated, shrink-wrapped, or other
  • Stackable or non-stackable
  • Liftgate needed at pickup or delivery
  • Inside pickup or inside delivery needed
  • Ready date and any special instructions
